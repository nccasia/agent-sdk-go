# Enrollment regulations

## Reservation of study (bảo lưu)
A student may reserve their study results for up to **two semesters** (12 months total).
Submit the reservation request at least **7 working days** before the semester start.
A reserved student keeps their completed credits and resumes at the same tuition tier.

## Withdrawal
A withdrawal within the first **14 days** of a course is fee-free. After 14 days, the
tuition for the current course is non-refundable but future courses are released.

## Transfer between programs
A program transfer is allowed once per academic year. Completed credits that map to the
new program carry over; unmapped credits are archived, not refunded.

## Examinations
A student must attempt the final exam within **30 days** of finishing the course material.
One free re-sit is allowed; a second re-sit costs a re-examination fee (see pricing).
