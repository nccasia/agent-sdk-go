# Tuition and pricing

## Tuition tiers
- **Foundation tier**: 4,500,000 VND per course.
- **Professional tier**: 6,000,000 VND per course.
- **Specialization tier**: 7,500,000 VND per course.

## Payment schedule
Tuition is due in two installments: 50% before the course starts and 50% at the midpoint.
A 5% discount applies when the full course tuition is paid up front.

## Refunds
A fee-free withdrawal within 14 days (see regulations) refunds 100% of any paid tuition
for that course. After 14 days no tuition refund is issued for the current course.

## Re-examination fee
A second re-sit of a final exam costs **300,000 VND** per attempt.
